
# ABSTRACT: Level 9 AI Safety Audit Framework

**PROJECT:** High-Dimensional Entropy Audit for Mission-Critical AI Systems  
**CORE METRIC:** Jensen-Shannon Divergence (JSD)  
**OBJECTIVE:** Mitigate AI Sycophancy and Hallucinations in Defense and Health-Tech.

## Executive Summary
The Level 9 AI Audit framework introduces a mathematical operational boundary to prevent Large Language Models (LLMs) from becoming "echo chambers" or providing submissive, biased responses (Sycophancy). 

By mapping interactions into high-dimensional vector spaces and calculating the **Jensen-Shannon Divergence (JSD)**, the system enforces a minimum threshold of cognitive friction. This ensures that the AI functions as a rigorous, independent interlocutor, strictly adhering to empirical data over user-induced subjective perceptions.

## Key Technical Features:
*   **Zero-Hallucination Entropy Audit:** Independent of internal model states.
*   **Stochastic Normalization:** Transforms text vectors into valid probability distributions.
*   **Compliance Ready:** Designed to meet the 'High-Risk' requirements of the EU AI Act.
*   **Mission-Critical Safety:** Prevents catastrophic feedback loops in defense and clinical environments.
